System & Components List

A robust system is to be designed describing the layout of component interaction and general structure of the arm (10/23)

×

CAD & Printing

The arm will be 3D printed for weight considerations. Base design, arm linkages, and gripper must all be designed in CAD (11/8)

×

R&D for PCB

Significant research is necessary in our development of stepper motor driver PCBs. Schematics and datasheets for existing stepper motor drives will be referenced and modified to create our own boards (11/31)

×

Component Testing

After the design phase of our project, we will shift to system integration which will start confirming the feasibility of our system design by testing our components mesh with each other (11/18)

×

Arm Assembly

Upon confirmation of our components being viable for our use, we will assemble our full system and iterate to create our final assembly (11/20)

×

Calibrate Arm

Map controller buttons and calibrate analog inputs for smooth motor movements (11/23)

×

Inverse Kinematics

Inverse kinematics must be derived to simplify the operation of the arm and end-effector. These kinematics will greatly reduce the cognitive load of the operator of the arm (11/29)

×

Testing & Iteration

After full system assembly and calibration, we will move onto testing our system to gauge its functionality and limitations along with tweaking the system for best performance (12/1)
